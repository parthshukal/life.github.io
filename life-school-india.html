Is this a degree course or a diploma? Does the Mumbai University recognize the syllabus?

Our philosophy is entirely different from the University of Mumbai. Hence, they won’t authorize Life-School. We believe in the traditional Indian education like Vedic math and so on. The Indian way of life and learning has been appreciated by Ivy Leagues colleges too, and we also don't feel reluctant in adapting the case-study based learning approach from the Western, Ivy League system. The syllabus is a blend of - the likes of Harvard with the Indian Ved, Arthshastra, and Chanakya Neeti!

We neither offer a certificate nor a diploma; it is Life (School) - which believes in building your lives rather than your resumes.

When you’ve already earned one graduate degree from the Mumbai University, why look for more certificates? Now, it’s time for some real learning!

What would the intake process be in terms of the Aptitude Test, GD-PI, and difficulty of English?

Meet Ved. He can't debate well because this is his first time. Ved isn't a native English speaker but is trying hard to learn English vocabulary. Shall we reject Ved? We might miss out on a potential artist, a prospective strategist, a potential mathematician, a potential analyst.

The approach of testing intellect through vocabulary, general discussions or general knowledge is not our fashion. If Indian institutes focused more on bringing out the gem in a person, his intelligence wouldn't have been measured through his entrance exam percentiles.

What is the teaching approach?

Teachers here help you apply your classroom learning in real-world settings. You don't have to be embarrassed about not knowing things or learning at a snail’s pace. Six months ago you may have thought that you were slow in a subject, but later you shall feel that you're gifted.

The faculties conduct ongoing research and writing. They often involve students in their research and guide students to undertake independent research projects. They will work alongside you, challenge and question you, share your triumphs, encourage you to do better. Over the course, your professors will become your mentor and friends.

Will I get help for higher level work?

During the internship, we don't get irritated by people who don’t know things, but by people who don’t want to learn them. Here, your buddy loves to answer your question. 'Buddy’ since there’s no boss, no sir. For us, what you 'could do' will always be more important than what you 'have done'.

What would be my profile in terms of the learning curve?

The information that we study shall help in the valuation of the company and in forecasting its finances from a portfolio manager's perspective. These activities would come under:

  • Investment Banking: IPO valuation, M&A (Synergy Valuation that companies like Morgan Stanley & J P Morgan do)
  • Equity Research & Valuation (that companies like Motilal Oswal do)
  • Consulting (that firms like Mckinsey and BCG do)


The technical knowledge we'd impart and apply: industry/company analysis, growth stories, evaluation of competitor strategies, company financials, stock valuation, the impact of macroeconomic factors. Also, founders’ growth story and vision, different management styles through case studies of successful entrepreneurs like Dhirubhai Ambani, Steve Jobs, Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, Warren Buffett, and so on.

In our tech week, we’d analyze top international players like Tata, Facebook, Google, Apple, Samsung, Yahoo, Tesla, etc. This practical analysis would complement the current education system.

We teach you essential soft skills like how to dress as per the event, positive Body Language, Effective Communication (which includes words, silence, and actions), and so on. Do you know the ideal distance to keep while interacting with someone senior? Or how to hand over a visiting card to a client? What is the perfect handshake?

What type of networking opportunities will I have?

Network through our speaker series where scholars (academicians), industry professionals including experts from Investment Banking, Equity Research Analysts, Portfolio Managers, consultants shall interact with you on topics of significance.

Seminars and events: high tea parties, business luncheons, brunch meetings, sessions on corporate etiquette for table manners for supper and client dinners. studying. The remaining shall be spent applying what you’ve learned.

Will it help me get a better job/internship later?

Knowledge is the edge. For us, the reward is not your work, but your learning since we are charging you. The other internships might pay you because the reward for them is your work. They aren't running a training school.

Naturally there, the work quality is dependent on your experience, track record, vintage, qualification and so on. The work you shall do here with us is core finance similar to senior analysts. This is what matters in the next interview you go to because companies prefer hiring gutsy professionals with hands-on startup experience.

Will this be part time or full time? Is it tiring?

There's no end time. Go back when you are tired, take an off on a day you don't feel like coming. Though the work will be so exciting that you’d want to continue working. We are open to, working at flexible times. We want you to learn. Less than half of the time would be studying. The remaining shall be spent applying what you’ve learned.

How much travel will be involved?

Even though you are not in a sales role, selling your ideas and presenting a research report is what every financial analyst/ investment banker does for their investors. Travelling between Nariman Point-BKC and back to Worli and Lower Parel would be a part of your role.
